U-tena art Mentorship

Under this program U-tena recruits young people from any background and trains them in performing art and life skills.This is a mentorship program to develop artists to become active members within their own community as well as a link for them to do commercial shows to sustain their own livelihoood.

PERFORMANCE(IGA)

U-TENA uses art/performance as a way to teach and influence behaviour change.Additionally performance is an income generating activity to support programming.
