Become A Volunteer

U-Tena is a community based non-profit organization.Members work collectively developing and delivering U-Tena programs and performances.All community outreach performances are unpaid.Funds for U-Tena come from small project grants, performances and donations.

U-Tena does not currently have secure funding for core programs and administration.We graciously accept donations in form of money or materials.Our wishlist include, uniforms, cleats for year 12-14 football teams , costumes and props for theatre performances, musical instruments, computers, and audio visual equipment.
